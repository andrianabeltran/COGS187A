Consistency and standards

Users should not have to wonder whether different words, situations, or actions mean the same thing. Follow platform conventions.

Below are the four level two pages featured on the front page. It is possible to identify a number of difference across them.

  1. Background and navigation bar color is different across all pages, although it can be considered as different design choice.
  2. The top part including the logo and search field is consistent across all pages.
  3. On the static navigation bar, page 1 and 2 uses the word "Home", while page 3 and 4 use a different word "University Home".
  4. The way to present information is inconsistent between page 1, 2 and 3/4.
