Recognition rather than recall

Minimize the user's memory load by making objects, actions, and options visible. The user should not have to remember information from one part of the dialogue to another. Instructions for use of the system should be visible or easily retrievable whenever appropriate.

For most of the pages, current position is indicated on two dynamic navigation bar, with directories listed, such that the users from do not need to recall where he/she may have came from. Navigation bar A and B are corresponded with each other, but nav. bar B takes a step further and display more articles within the same directory.
