Match between system and the real world

The system should speak the users' language, with words, phrases and concepts familiar to the user, rather than system-oriented terms. Follow real-world conventions, making information appear in a natural and logical order.

Most of the words and phrases are commonly used, so there isn't a problem, but there are a small number of terms that are confusing.

One such example is the term "Distance Learning." "Distance Learning" could be confused with "International Students," as they both refer to learning not on campus. It might be that it is just a term commonly used in the UK, but could be confusing for students out of the country.

Another example is the term "Spin-outs". It is not obvious after navigating from "Technology transfer" what the term "Spin-outs" means, although they do define it on the page itself though.
