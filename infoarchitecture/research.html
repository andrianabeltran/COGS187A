Resolving Global Challenges

The University's research plays an important role in a changing world. From the invention of genetic fingerprinting, pioneering space explorations through to our search for King Richard III - our work is world leading and impactful. Our Global Challenges are a way in which this work from across the University comes together to really make a difference to everyone involved in the process and the world beyond.


Research Institutes

9 research centres, with over 100 active research groups. From science and engineering, to arts and humanities. Here at the University of Leicester, our goal is to push the human race forward.

Office of Research

As a major research university in the country, the school administration is fully supportive toward any research activities that aims to solve challenging questions.

Research Archive

University's Open Access repository of research publications and theses database. Open Access increases the visibility of your research. Find out how to contribute your publications and submit your thesis.

This is us

The very compact campus contains a wide range of twentieth century architecture, though the oldest building, the Fielding Johnson building, dates from 1837. The main campus is a mile south of the city centre, adjacent to Victoria Park and Wyggeston and Queen Elizabeth I College.
